About me


Over the years I have learned that wealth is created by saving every penny. The moment I started saving a bit of money, I thought of buying random stuff, like everyone one else would. But them I thought, do I actually need any thing new?

This forced me to evaluate everything that I possesed, was it worth replacing?

Like for example, as of today, I have used my
macbook pro for almost three years, without a single repair or trouble.
iPhone 6 for almost 2 years, without a trouble.
Sennheisers Headphones for almost four years, without it loosing its quality.
Wildcraft bag for almost 2 years, without a single wear or tear.

This made me realise I don't need to buy stuff again and again, if I buy from high quality producers.

Some products are made to make your life easier.


I firmly believe that once we buy a product, we should make complete use of it, before it wears out.
And I hate it when something breaks or stops functioning mid-use when I have not used it to my full content.


On the other hand, there are some products which are there by your side for life.

As a consumer, I know you would prefer to buy products which are long lasting, worth every penny, and most of all, useful.

On this site, I have showcased some such products.

Do enjoy these products.
